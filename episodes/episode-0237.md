We dive into the 2025 'State of Dev in Morocco' survey results! Join us as we analyze the latest trends in the Moroccan tech scene. We discuss the impact of AI on development, the debate between specialization and full-stack skills, the state of tech education, the rise of entrepreneurship, remote work preferences, and the role of community. What's next for Moroccan developers? Let's find out!
