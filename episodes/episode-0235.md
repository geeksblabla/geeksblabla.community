Dive deep into the OWASP Top 10 web vulnerabilities with a panel of six cybersecurity experts. This episode breaks down each major risk, from Broken Access Control to Server-Side Request Forgery. Learn how these flaws are exploited and, more importantly, how to secure your applications. A must-watch for developers and security pros!
